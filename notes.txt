+Seed of the Day race

Player follows straight stretch that is smooth, and easy to drive on
	-rewards speed not tilting accuracy
	-momentum is important and makes the player feel "fun"

After straight stretches, player then hits "obstacle sections"
	-straight sections of track with obstacles the players must dodge
	-obstacles can move, can be holes in the track, or any other number
	 of devious things
	-obstacle sections are designed to slow players down a fair bit,
	 and allow marbles to "catch up" to one another and experience
	 a fun close quarters race.
